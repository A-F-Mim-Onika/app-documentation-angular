Overview

We appreciate your interest in our FoodApp.

FoodApp is a simplified and customizable single restaurant food delivery system, developed using Flutter framework. Flutter is an open-source framework by Google for building beautiful, natively compiled, multi-platform applications from a single codebase. We have already completed the groundwork for you – relieving you of the stress of small detail.

FoodApp consists of 2 Mobile Applications and 1 Admin Panel –

  • Customer application for Android and iOS using Flutter.
  • Delivery application for delivery person, for Android and iOS, developed using Flutter.
  • Admin panel/management dashboard for application and order management for both Android and iOS using Flutter.

The Happy Flow

Here, we will explain how FoodApp works for customers, delivery persons and restaurant. From easy of order placement to delivery tracking, we will show every step of how this application works.

Order Flow

  • Customer adds an item to the cart, checks out and confirms the order.
  • Restaurant receives the order in the order receive section and confirms the order.
  • Order will be processed by the restaurant then a delivery person will be assigned.
  • Delivery person picks up the order from the restaurant and be on his way to deliver to the customer.

Dispatch Flow

  • Restaurant receives the order, confirms it.
  • Restaurant cooks the order.
  • Admin looks for available delivery person from admin panel and assigns the order.
  • Assigned delivery person picks up the order from the restaurant.

Transaction Flow

Here, we will show the calculation of earnings for restaurant, delivery person and commission.

  • Food value = Original food price - (Food discount + Coupon discount for food)
  • Order value = (Food value + Tax/Vat (on food value) + Delivery Charge) – Coupon discount for deliver charge
  • Customer can’t apply more than one coupon for an order.
  • Tax/VAT is totally restaurant’s earning and restaurant needs to manage Tax/VAT manually for the government.

Cash Flow

  • For digital transaction, the order amount will be added to admin wallet.
  • For cash on delivery (COD), the order amount will be added to COD wallet in delivery person’s wallet.

Unique Features

  • Special offers can be easily accessed from home page.
  • Most popular items can be easily viewed from home page.
  • Bookmark your favorite items.
  • Customize certain toppings according to your desire.
  • Check your past orders anytime for bookkeeping, or repeat your order(s) from the past.
